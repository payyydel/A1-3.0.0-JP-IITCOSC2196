Find an advertisement showing what you believe to be your ideal job. This may require several years of experience, and hence be something that you must work towards, rather than something that you are ready for now, or will be able to fill as soon as you graduate.


Software Engineer - Apple Australia



A description of the position, and particularly what makes this position appealing to you.

The position requires you to execute and maintain a high quality of code and by extension security, integrity and performance surrounding certain applications. Utilising the likes of CI/CD, GitHub and Jenkins as tools. Debugging and troubleshooting abilities are a requirement, and the need to meet deadlines is a key aspect of the job. A desire to learn and excel is a must. And with that comes responsibility and personal pride in your work.

The most appealing part of this job, aside from the opportunity to work on projects that are used by the public day in and out, is the value placed on responsibility and integrity. Fostering a healthy environment that promotes transparent and fair collaboration can often sound like a pipe dream to some. But the level of innovation that Apple is working on, coupled with this relevant presence in today’s economy means that you’re held accountable. For not only any shortcomings, and they do happen, we’re only human after all, but for all of the wins. The element of uncertainty, mixed with pressure, whilst striving to keep an equilibrium of sorts means that you’d be challenged every day you step off the lift. What’s most reassuring is Apple’s commitment to equal opportunity. Regardless of race, religion, gender or political standpoint. Everyone is guaranteed the same opportunity as those. And are treated as such. Makes for a very unique point of view when attacking issues head-on.


A description (in your own words) of the skills, qualifications and experience required for the position.

Proven capacity in Java/Java EE and front and back-end services ranging from XML, JSON, HTTP and SSL, to JavaScript, HTML, CSS and all-encompassing JS frameworks. Additionally, experience and competencies in SFDC Apex, Visualforce, Lightning web components, relational databases, data modelling, object-oriented programming and design patterns. Development environments like agile and scrum will also be in play.


A description of the skills, qualifications and experience you currently have.

My current capacity surrounding the requirements for this role is truthfully limited. I have rudimentary experience with XML, JSON and HTTP. I’m familiar with JavaScript, HTML and CSS, however, that is currently the extent of my ability.


A plan describing how you will obtain the skills, qualifications and experience required for the position, building on those you have now.

Starting with this undergraduate degree in Information Technology, I plan to take my knowledge and investigate additional studies surrounding Cyber Security and general Computer Sciences. Whilst studying I have already begun to teach myself coding languages, starting with back to basics with JavaScript, HTML and CSS. Reinforcing my foundations is a strategy I’m employing because I want to be confident in my ability. I believe, that in order to demonstrate my impending expertise, I’ll need to be confident in my capabilities first. I also plan to begin building a portfolio and developing projects that are close to my heart. The likes of which you’ll be able to review in the “Project Ideas” section of this site.
