16 Personalities | Myers-Briggs



What do the results of these tests mean for you?

I believe self-awareness is an asset. A tool to be delicately wielded. These results simply articulate my inner workings and allow me the resources to make informed and healthy decisions.


How do you think these results may influence your behaviour in a team?

Minimally. A bolstered ego isn’t really in line with my personality type, nor does it play a part in my personal demeanour. The only impact I believe these results may have is a reaffirming one. The results are accurate in that I place a great deal of value in a team that fosters equal opportunity and free-flowing ideas. Communication that is clear and empathetic towards a person’s particular point of view is a must. Furthermore, conflict isn’t a particularly strong suite; however, I do believe that in a professional environment, honesty is the best policy.


How should you take this into account when forming a team?

Honesty, upfront. Honesty when it comes to explaining my capabilities, any life circumstances that may inadvertently have an impact on my team, and its work.
