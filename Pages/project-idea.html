Overview

My project will be a website and accompanying app platforms. The purpose of this site will be to host a centralised voting hub and journalistic/forum-based news and reviews platform. Specifically surrounding streaming sites and primarily original content produced and released by them. Allowing audiences to have their honest say and virtually transforming the way streaming original shows and films are picked up by companies. The goal is to partner will all major and zone-specific streaming companies in an effort to provide them with a means by which to hear from audiences, rather than relying solely on initial viewing numbers.



Motivation

An ever-growing industry, with no signs of stopping “the streaming industry is expected to be worth $330 billion by 2030”. A number of platforms have entered the market from nearly all major motion picture studios and networks, and the COVID-19 pandemic only built momentum in terms of subscriber growth. However, the likes of titans such as Netflix have lost north of 1.1 million subscribers since the start of 2022. Reasons multiply the more you read about it, but one obvious and relatable factor is the “quality of content” on the platform. Netflix is a pioneer in the medium of online streaming platforms; capitalising on that, they began producing and releasing original content on their platforms globally, with notable fan favourites like Stranger Things and 13 Reasons Why. But lately, they’ve been lacking in content consumers want to see, instead opting to cancel numerous shows after only 1 pilot season runs.

All this commotion has called into question why all these shows and film franchises are being scrapped before they have a chance to truly soar. Unfortunately, controversy is corrosive, and people are stubborn. Questions like these are already being asked of platforms that are, by Netflix’s standards, effectively in their infancy. The likes of Disney+, Hulu, Amazon Prime Video, Paramount+, HBO Max and so forth are having their content placed under scrutiny, and their decisions even more so. With “change.org” petitions and Reddit-specific forums gaining more and more traffic with every cancellation, or controversial approval.

Furthermore, this will present a particular challenge and opportunity for budding and tenured directors, writers, actors etc. that they can rise to. Short-form video has already been popularised through the likes of social media apps like TikTok and Instagram. Attempting to communicate and compel people in a single or limited series run format, will really allow creatives to put their best foot forward. The bar has been raised, and the masses have higher standards than ever before. As such, the need for a centralised space by which to discuss, review, view and vote is long overdue.

Tools and Technologies

  • Creating a fully autonomous website will require coding languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Code editors such as Visual Code Studio, Atom, Sublime Text etc. will also be necessary for supporting code development, updates, and maintenance.
  • A user-interface framework like “Twitter Bootstrap” will be helpful in creating code variations as well as simplifying and speed-up front-end development.
  • Using data structure repositories through version control platforms such as GitHub will be paramount in the initial construction and further maintaining the platform.
  • Version control platforms like GitHub also provide developers with a mountain of support, with intentional design features and functionality to promote seamless collaboration.
  • Maintaining copies of all active and archived files will be a priority. Using the “3 2 1 Backup” method, usage of two on-site, independent read device formats will be employed. Including local drives, and external drives. And one online cloud-based solution such as “IDrive”, “Backblaze” or “SpiderOak One”.
  • In addition to backup solutions, file sharing services will also be required. The likes of Microsoft One Drive, Google Drive and Dropbox can be used. I aim to stay away from more personal cloud solutions like iCloud, simply because those types of platforms are designed around consumer needs and are, by extension, less than ideal surrounding support, flexibility, and security.
  • Graphic design programs to assist in concept creation and design implementation will also be required. I’ve found the Adobe CC Suite to be a great place to start, boasting a wealth of versatile and familiar programs. Lightroom and Photoshop are most popular and relied upon.
  • Content creation in general will be relevant in creating a really unique brand and environment. Leaning again on Adobe for programs like Premier Pro, After Effects etc. Alternate options can include Avid Media Composer for video editing, Unreal Engine for animation or environment creation, or even Da Vinci Resolve for film and animation-based colour grading.
  • Font sourcing will also be required in order to support the graphic elements. Widely accepted and contributed to libraries such as Adobe Fonts and Microsoft Font/Typography library will be useful repositories to pull from.
  • Additional requirements may include employment of pre-processors (SASS), markup validators (HTML Validator), automation support (Gulp), page speed testers (Google PageSpeed) etc.


Skills Required

Experience in front-end coding languages, navigation and usage of different backup processes and cloud-based file sharing services, familiarity with version control platforms, design composition and execution with industry-standard programs, general resourcefulness and troubleshooting skills surrounding asset sourcing and code maintenance etc. and knowledge of prelaunch processes, statistics, and validations in order to launch and integrate new sections or platforms. Acquiring all these skills will be a career-building initiative, requiring dedication and development in new areas and skills. Undergraduate degrees will require 3 years minimum of study time, although all these processes can be self-taught.



Outcome

If successful, this initiative will effectively change the way streaming companies can approach original content productions, saving themselves potentially millions, and bringing profit margins back up to scratch. Imagine being able to view pilot episodes, or limited series runs, which are in essence teasers for potential shows. Initial productions would be much shorter. While waiting for audiences and journalists alike to review and vote on content. Turnaround times will be a factor, but with more devoted resources, and a launch period, there will be plenty of content to boot in the meantime. Content people actually want to see.

Also, allowing everyday audiences to have their say, and shot, at being heard; whilst also keeping them informed on all the variables, in an effort to make to most responsible and ethically sound decisions. Entertainment that’s made for the people and chosen and informed by the people has the potential to open up a world of possibilities. Hopefully, one day melting the borders between what could’ve been, what shouldn’t be and what will be. Inspiring and empowering people to share honest opinions.
